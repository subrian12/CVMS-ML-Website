Our coaches

Our coaching staff is made up of highly qualified CVMS Math League alumni with backgrounds in competitive mathematics. All have participated in a wide range of math contests, gaining experience and learning advanced problem-solving techniques. As former members of the program, they are able to provide informed and relevant guidance.

Our mission

Mathematics is the best playground to learn pattern recognition, problem-solving, and abstract thinking. Our mission is to provide the needed friendly environment for students to explore mathematics together under the guidance of our experienced coaches. We foster curiosity, confidence, and creativity in our students.
